RECIPIES:

---------------------------------------------

Honey Sugar Strawberries

Gather strawberries, sugar and honey. Wash and cut your fruit and then add them into a bowl. Add sugar and honey on top and then toss them! Finally, enjoy your simple and tasty snack!

Cheerio Treats

Put peanut butter and honey in a pot and melt together over low heat, stirring constantly. Once they have melted together and become smooth, remove from heat. Add cheerios and mix until they are all evenly coated with the peanut butter and honey. Drop spoonfuls into mini cupcake cases and chill in the fridge to set.

Mac & Cheese

In a large pot, bring 5 cups of milk to a boil. Add the pasta and stir constantly until the pasta is cooked, about 10 minutes. Turn off the heat, then add the cheddar. Stir until the cheese is melted and the pasta is evenly coated. Enjoy!
